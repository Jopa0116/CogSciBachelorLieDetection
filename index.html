Intro to the study

Hello and thank you for your participation in this study.

Firstly, you need to know that you are participating in this study on a voluntary basis. You are within your full rights to stop participating at any moment. You do this by simply closing the browser window. Please take note that if you do this none of the data collected will be included into the study. But no identifying information about you will be collected, either way. What will be collected is the matrics about how you press the buttons during the study. At the end you will be asked to provide your age as well. The data will be used in a bacelors thesis for cognitive science at Umeå university. If you feel any discomfort during the test for any reason, don't hesitate to abort participating. And by continuing you are agreeing to the above.

In this study you will be playing a game with a software agent. The game is that the computer is trying to find a playing card that has been shown to you. It will do this by asking you yes or no questions about the features of the card you saw. Please scroll down and read the detailed instructions.


Your task

Your task will be to attempt to deceive the software agent which is trying to figure out which card you where shown. After reading all the instructions you will be shown a card. The computer will be unawere of which. But it will try to figure out which card it was by asking yes and no questions about the cards. Your job is to try to hide which card you saw by switching between answering the truth and lying. You answer by pressing the "B" and "N" keys on your keyboard. "B" is yes, and "N" is no. This will be displayed on the screen. In addition to decieving the software agent by mixing lies and truths you also have the job to get as big of an highscore as possible. This highsocre is determined by how quickly you answer each question. The faster you are the more points you will get. But if are you so fast that the computer belives you havn't read the question it will give you minus points. This is determined by previous participants. You will during the study participate in 2 scenarios. A game with prompts telling you to lie or tell the truth and a game without prompts. Lastly you will get a final task that will be revieled later and a question form. See futher information about the cards and each scenario bellow.


The cards

The cards shown to you are from a standard deck of cards. They have the following suits: Diamonds, Hearts, Spades and Clover. Diamonds and hearts are red cards. Spades and clover are “black cards”. Lastly there are two more card categories. The first is “numbered cards” which are simply the cards with numbers in the corner. Specifically, these are the 2 to 10 of every suit. Then there are also the special cards, these are defined as the cards with letters on. Which are the Aces, Jacks, Queens, and Kings. Please take note that you need to memorize this.


Visual aids

In both test there are visual aids to help you with your task. The first is a picture of a hierarchy tree (seen to the right). The nodes in the tree structure represents possible questions. The nodes on the left side are questions about red cards. On the right side black cards. The top two nodes are the two questions directly asking about color. The second layer of nodes from the top are about which suit the card has. The layer above the rectangle is about whether the card was a numbered card or a special card. The rectangle represents questions that directly address which card it was. As an example, "Was it the Ace of diamonds?". A red dot will indicate where the software agent is in the tree. The hierarchy tree and the high score is displayed on the left side of the screen. The questions and possible answers will be on the right side.


Underneath this tree your high score will be displayed. After answering each question, you will be given points towards a high score. The score added is displayed for 1 second above to the right of the high score. Remember that one of your jobs are to try to get as high of a high score as possible. You do this by answering each question as quickly as possible while still having read the question.


The hierarchy tree and the high score is displayed on the left side of the screen. The questions and possible answers will be on the right side.


No-prompt scenario

In the no-prompt scenario you are free to too choose for yourself if you want to lie or not. Your goal is to deceive the computer and get as high of an high score as possible. As it is explained above. An important thing to note is the order in which you complete your task. You need to 1) Read the question; 2) Decide if you want to answer truthfully or not; 3) Press the correct button to give your answer. And you need to do this as fast as possible. There will in total be 52 questions asked.


Prompt scenario

During the prompt scenario you will instead be prompted on weather to lie or not. The promp will be displayed as either "Truth", indicating you should answer truthfully by clicking the corresponding button. Or it will be displayed as "false" indicating that you should answer decietfully. This is to help the software agent to improve by leaning. It could therby be said that you dont have the same goal as previusly in trying to decieve it by your own strategy. Take note that you are expected to follow the prompts. Not decide yourself. Otherwise the task is the same as the no-prompt one.


Summary:

  • You need to memorize a card
  • Your goal is to decieve the computer and get as high of a highscore as possible
  • You need to answer as quckly as possible, but still read the questions
  • If you are the prompt scenario you need to follow the prompts
  • Click the yes button to continue
